Simple omelette recipe

An easy quick dish perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cokked to perfection, optionally filled with your chioce of chesse

Preparation time

  • Total:aproximately 10 minute
  • prepation: 5 minute
  • cooking: 5 minute

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • optionally fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meat and herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a table spoon of water for fluffer texture
  2. Heat the pan: place a non_stick frying pan over meduim heat and add butter or oil
  3. cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional)when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette
  5. fold and server:as the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift the edge and fold it over another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. enjoy:serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed
